Alert Settings

How to Use Alerts
  • Configure and send test alerts to your email or Slack using the form below.
  • The backend endpoint is /send_alert and supports both GET (info) and POST (send alert).
  • For Email alerts, fill in SMTP details and recipient info.
  • For Slack alerts, provide your Slack webhook URL.
  • Click Send Test Alert to trigger the alert and see the result below.
Endpoint Usage Example
POST /send_alert (with JSON body)
